ABOUT US






Beginning from humble roots in 2011 as a fashion photography company, Dinuka Vidushan photography expanded with time to tailor services to cover a diverse portfolio of Model, Event, and Wedding photography. Over the span of two decades, our team has had the honour of capturing mesmerizing memories behind lenses across 14 different countries in over 2000 different shoots. Satisfying over 4000 clients and adding a touch of finesse to their special day, we as a team have created a positive perception of the client generating an optimistic word of mouth that has helped us sustain in the long run.
